Welcome

Welcome to SweatyRS - a rotation planner and DPM calculator built for people who look at RuneScape combat and think, "yeah, I can optimize that harder."

This started as a passion project to help me learn Ranged PvM, but also because RuneScape combat math is an absolute mess and apparently I enjoy suffering. Right now this is still an MVP, so it only supports Ranged. If there's demand, I'll keep adding more mechanics, more cleanup, and more combat styles.

One important disclaimer: do not blindly trust every number on this site. RuneScape math is cursed, the game explains half of nothing, and a lot of these calculations come from testing, assumptions, experience, and educated guesswork held together by caffeine and bad decisions.

If you want to help the project, the best thing you can do is share it around. The second best thing is to support it with a donation, which is a very effective way of telling me this tool deserves even more unhealthy development hours.

I've been playing RuneScape for over 20 years, and this is my little contribution to the community. Have fun, and go build something disgusting.

Workflow

Recommended order

  1. Build the loadout and backpack in Gear.
  2. Enable persistent effects in Buffs.
  3. Review abilities if needed in Abilities.
  4. Place the rotation in Rotation Planner.
  5. Inspect totals and hit breakdowns in Results.
